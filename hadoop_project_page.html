How Hadoop Connects to System Meltdown

The simulator’s “forbidden nodes” and “mission‑critical nodes” mirror real Hadoop cluster behavior:

  • ZooKeeper = the brain (forbidden to touch)
  • NameNode = single point of truth
  • DataNodes = safe to operate on
  • YARN = orchestrator of distributed tasks
